Welcome to V-Runs Community

Our running club offers a supportive and motivating environment for runners of all levels. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned pro, join us for group runs, training tips, and a friendly community to share your passion for running.
FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How can I join the Vishakhapatnam Running Club? Joining our club is easy! Simply visit our website and fill out the membership form under the "Join Us" section. You can also connect with us on social media for updates on membership registration events
Do I need to be an experienced runner to join? Not at all! We welcome runners of all levels, from beginners to seasoned athletes. Whether you're just starting out or looking to improve your performance, there's a place for you in our club.
Where and when do you typically organize group runs? We organize group runs at various locations across Vishakhapatnam, including beaches, parks, and scenic trails. The schedule for group runs is posted on our website and social media channels
Can non-members participate in your club's events? Some of our events may be open to non-members, but priority registration and discounts are typically offered to club members. Check our event announcements for specific details on participation requirements.
